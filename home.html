ABOUT PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE

Introduction to PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE

PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE was established as a shoe store Sendirian Berhad in June 2019 under the Shops Act in Malaysia. This store opened with the name of PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE which is thought to be a step forward in the future. PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE was formed from the brainchild of young people. A Bumiputra shop complete with modest capital. PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE Headquarters located at No 83 Lot Bangunan MDM Pekan Wakaf Tapai 21040 Marang, Terengganu Darul Iman. Contact us at 0113-5400297 and can email to palmastella.gmail.com.

The PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE headquarters has provided facilities for administrative and business activities. It is equipped with a shoe showroom, service activities at the inquiry counter, administrative matters and others. PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE sells items such as sports shoes, walking shoes, office shoes, women's shoes, formal shoes, children's shoes and more. The brands we sell are like Adidas, Nike, Puma, Converse, Vans, Dr.Martens, Clark, Men's Shoes, Smart Shoes, High Heels, and many more which are some of the main shoe items of our store. on other shoe accessories shoes such as shoe soles, insole shoes, shoe polish, shoelaces and others. PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE sells designer shoes in addition to affordable prices that can be bought by all walks of life and can also help low-income people to buy shoes at reasonable prices. Shoes sold in this store have been approved by the Ministry of Commerce and also include the ISO9001 Certificate, as well as International Recognition. With strong problems, PALMASTELLA BOUTIQUE is now taking steps to open three new boutiques in selected locations by 2022.
